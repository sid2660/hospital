Presidency University Medical Center

Presidency University Medical Center is a tax supported community hospital located in north Presidency University near the Rajankuntay. Founded in 2008, this hospital has provided medical support to the community for over 12 years. Now located in a state of the art facility that opened in 2008, TMC continues to provide modern medical procedures to all members of the communities located in the hospital district. The hospital is controlled by a board whose membership is selected by the community by election. The hospital has a chief administrator that is responsible for the day to day operation of the facility.
